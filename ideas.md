# game ideas

---

## daily-decay (concept)

a girl's room. 7 days. each day you pick from 4 actions: "chat with friends", "draw", "listen to music", "look out the window". she smiles and reacts. her drawings and small items fill the room.

day 4: things shift. chat replies stop coming. drawings go dark. reactions fade. the text changes — "chat with friends" becomes "check if anyone read your message".

day 6: only 2 choices left. items disappear from the room. day 7: only "look out the window" remains.

final screen: empty room. open window. curtain blowing in the wind. instead of a score: "you stayed with her for 7 days".

## deploy (concept)

friday 17:59. you're alone. the deploy button pulses. 3 days of staging changes. risk gauge fluctuates — time your deploy when it's low. fail = late-night debugging minigame. chicken out = shame counter. survive 5 fridays.

---

## standup (concept)

10 AM. camera on. you did nothing yesterday. pick excuses. manager's BS detector rises. same excuse twice = suspicion spike. follow-up questions get sharper. survive monday–friday. twist: everyone was lying.

---

## 404 (next up)

top-down 2D horror. a small pixel character lost in the broken internet. dark — only a faint glow around the player. broken HTML fragments as walls and paths. navigate through escalating HTTP errors: 404 → 500 → 503 → ERR_CONNECTION_RESET. server hum ambient, glitch effects, a second cursor appears in later stages. reach index.html to escape. final screen: "200 OK" then "Welcome Home."
