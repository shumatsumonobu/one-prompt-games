## daily-decay (concept)

a girl's room. 7 days. each day you pick from 4 actions: "chat with friends", "draw", "listen to music", "look out the window". she smiles and reacts. her drawings and small items fill the room.

day 4: things shift. chat replies stop coming. drawings go dark. reactions fade. the text changes — "chat with friends" becomes "check if anyone read your message".

day 6: only 2 choices left. items disappear from the room. day 7: only "look out the window" remains.

final screen: empty room. open window. curtain blowing in the wind. instead of a score: "you stayed with her for 7 days".
